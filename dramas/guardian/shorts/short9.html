Why Gong Yoo's Casting Took 5 Years
Writer Kim Eun-sook spent five years trying to cast Gong Yoo. Having just completed his military service, Gong Yoo lacked the confidence to take on a star writer's project and kept hesitating. The result of that five-year wait was Goblin.
