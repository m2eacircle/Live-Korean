🎬 About This Drama
The Glory is a 2022–2023 Netflix Korean thriller drama starring Song Hye-kyo and Lee Do-hyun. Moon Dong-eun (Song Hye-kyo) suffered severe school bullying and violence as a teenager. After years of meticulous planning, she returns as an adult — as the homeroom teacher of her tormentor's child — to execute her long-awaited revenge.

The drama is praised for its sharp screenplay, Song Hye-kyo's powerful transformation, and its unflinching portrayal of the lasting damage caused by school violence. It became one of the most-watched Korean dramas on Netflix globally.

날카로운 각본, 송혜교의 강렬한 연기 변신, 학교 폭력의 상흔을 직시하는 묘사로 큰 호평을 받았습니다. 넷플릭스 역대 한국 드라마 중 가장 많이 시청된 작품 중 하나입니다.
